This app adds Anchorage People Mover buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule as well as the latest news from YouTube and @AncPeopleMover on Twitter.

People Mover buses serve Anchorage, Alaska, United States.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Municipality of Anchorage (MOA) and People Mover.
http://www.muni.org/Departments/transit/PeopleMover/Pages/GTFSDiscliamer.aspx

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/us-anchorage-people-mover-bus-android

This app is not related with the Municipality of Anchorage (MOA) and People Mover.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read the news from Twitter & YouTube
